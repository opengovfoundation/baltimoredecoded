                              BALTIMORE CITY CHARTER

NEW TERMS FOR BOARDS AND COMMISSIONS. (§ IX-8)

The first terms of office provided by the new Charter for members of boards and
commissions that
are coterminous with the Mayor’s term of office shall begin on the last day of
December, 1995, and
the first four year terms of office provided by the new Charter for board and
commission members
that are staggered with the Mayor’s term of office shall begin two years
thereafter.
