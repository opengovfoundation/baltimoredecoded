                              BALTIMORE CITY CHARTER

DEPARTMENT OF PLANNING: PENALTIES. (ยง VII-80)

Any person who shall violate any of the provisions or requirements of Sections
71 to 80 shall be
guilty of a misdemeanor, and, upon conviction in any court of competent
jurisdiction, shall be fined
as provided by law.  Every day that such violation continues shall be deemed a
separate offense.
