                              BALTIMORE CITY CHARTER

FINES AND PENALTIES. (ยง II-48)

To provide civil and criminal fines and penalties for the violation of any
ordinance, rule or
regulations established by the City under or in the execution of any power
granted by this Article II;
provided that no fine or penalty shall exceed $1000.00 and no imprisonment shall
be for a longer
period than 12 months.
(Ch. 524, Acts of 1982; Ch. 551, Acts of 1997.)
