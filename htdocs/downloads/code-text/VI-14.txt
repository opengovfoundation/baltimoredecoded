                              BALTIMORE CITY CHARTER

BONDS OF MUNICIPAL OFFICERS. (ยง VI-14)

Except as otherwise provided by State law, the Board of Estimates shall
determine which officials
of the City shall be bonded for the faithful performance of their duties and the
form and amount of
such bonds.  All premiums on official bonds shall be paid by the City.  All
bonds executed in favor
of the City shall be approved by the Mayor.
