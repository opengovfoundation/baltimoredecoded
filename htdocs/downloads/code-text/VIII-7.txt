                              BALTIMORE CITY CHARTER

RESERVATION OF RIGHTS. (ยง VIII-7)

When the grant of a franchise or right is made in compliance with the foregoing
sections, the City
shall not part with, but shall expressly reserve the right and duty at all times
to exercise in the interest
of the public full municipal superintendence, regulation and control in respect
to all matters
connected with said grant and not inconsistent with the terms thereof.
