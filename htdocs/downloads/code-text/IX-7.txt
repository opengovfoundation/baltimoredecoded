                              BALTIMORE CITY CHARTER

CONTRACTS AND OBLIGATIONS. (ยง IX-7)

All contracts, leases and obligations to which the City is a party at the
applicable effective date of the
new Charter shall remain in full force and effect according to their terms, and
shall be performed by
or for that municipal agency which, by the new Charter or applicable ordinances,
shall have
jurisdiction over the subject matter of such contracts, leases or obligations.
