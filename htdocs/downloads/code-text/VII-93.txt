The Board of Legislative Reference shall be comprised of
  the Mayor,
  the City Solicitor,
  the President of the Johns Hopkins University,
  the Deans of the University of Maryland and University of Baltimore Schools of
Law,
  a member of the City Council selected by the Council and
  the Director of the Enoch Pratt Library.
