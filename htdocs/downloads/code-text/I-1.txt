                              BALTIMORE CITY CHARTER

CORPORATE ENTITY. (§ I-1)

The inhabitants of the City of Baltimore are a corporation, by the name of the
“Mayor and City
Council of Baltimore,” and by that name shall have perpetual succession, may
sue and be sued, may
purchase and hold real, personal and mixed property and dispose of the same for
the benefit of the
City, as herein provided, and may have and use a common seal, which may be
altered at pleasure.
